About PulaTechConf 2025

Join us for an enriching experience in Pula, Croatia, from July 16th to 18th, 2025!

The Second Central and Mediterranean European Conference on New Technologies, Society, and Development builds upon the success of its inaugural edition in 2024. This conference continues to serve as a platform for collaboration and innovation across diverse scientific disciplines. Esteemed scholars and industry experts will gather to advance technological knowledge and explore its societal impacts.

Conference Highlights

  • Dynamic research presentations
  • Thought-provoking debates
  • Networking opportunities
  • Cooperation among professionals from various fields

Important Information

Abstract Deadline Extended: The new deadline for abstract submissions is June 25th. We will shortly send instructions for the full article submission to your registered email address.

Free Participation: Based on last year's experience, we are pleased to announce that participation in the conference will remain free of charge.

Review Process: Every submitted abstract will undergo a thorough peer-review process, and accepted abstracts will be published in the conference proceedings.

Useful Information During Conference

  • Name badges: Please wear your badge at all times during conference hours
  • Coffee & Water: Complimentary coffee and water will be provided at breaks. For ordering pizza for lunch you need to choose it on time on tablet
  • Info point: The info desk will be staffed throughout all sessions
  • Presentation uploads: If you're presenting, please send your slides to the organizers at least 2 hours before
  • Wi-Fi at Venues: Free Wi-Fi will be available via QR barcode on conference poster
