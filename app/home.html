Welcome to PulaTechConf 2026

Dates: July 15-17, 2026
Location: Pula, Croatia
Official website: TFPU 2026

Central and Mediterranean European Conference on New Technologies, Innovations, Society and Development

The third edition builds on the success of the 2025 conference and continues to serve as a platform for collaboration, research exchange, and innovation across scientific disciplines.

Esteemed scholars and industry experts will gather to advance technological knowledge and explore its societal impacts. The event will feature research presentations, debates, and networking opportunities, fostering cooperation among professionals from various fields.

The Call for Papers is available here. We cordially encourage abstract submissions through the conference submission portal. Accepted abstracts will be published in the conference proceedings.

About the Conference

The Central and Mediterranean European Conference on New Technologies, Society, and Development is scheduled to take place in Croatia in 2026. The conference serves as an important venue for collaboration and innovation across a wide range of scientific subjects, bringing together scholars and industry professionals to advance technological understanding.

Conference Topics

  • Emerging Technologies, Innovations, Health and Society
  • Society and Technological Integration
  • Sustainable Development through Technology
  • Industry 4.0 and the Future of Work
  • Digital Transformation in Various Sectors
  • Collaborative Innovation and Research Synergies
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Applications
  • Technological Education and Knowledge Sharing
  • Impact of Technology on Development

Keynote Speakers

Mag. Maja Dragan

FH Joanneum, Graz, Austria

Dr. Maja Dragan's research focuses on industrial automation, smart manufacturing, and cyber-physical systems. She has published more than 50 peer-reviewed articles and serves as Principal Investigator for the Horizon Europe project SmartFactory-ID.

Assoc. Prof. Hasan Sözen

University of Ordu, Turkey

Dr. Hasan Sözen works in machine learning, computer vision, and pattern recognition. He has co-authored more than 45 scientific publications and coordinates a major TÜBİTAK national research initiative.

Assoc. Prof. Žiga Emeršič

University of Ljubljana, Slovenia

Dr. Žiga Emeršič researches generative AI, deep learning, and biometrics. He has co-authored more than 60 research papers and is Deputy Lead of the EU Horizon project OnMoveID.

Getting Around Pula

  • Walking: The city center is compact and walkable.
  • Public transport: City buses connect the main city areas.
  • Taxi: Available throughout the city, with higher demand during tourist season.

What to See in Pula

Pula Arena

A Roman amphitheatre from the 1st century and one of Pula's most recognizable landmarks.

Temple of Augustus & Forum

The ancient Roman square in the old town center, ideal for a short walk between sessions.

Peak Season Notice: July is high tourist season in Pula. Please plan accommodation, parking, and local travel in advance.
