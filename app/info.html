Entrance Information

Coworking Pula - Main Entrance

Go straight through the entrance to the reception, then immediately turn left to find the elevator. In the elevator, press the button for the second floor, and when you exit, the event room will be right in front of you.

If you are coming by stairs, please speak to the person at the reception desk who will direct you.

Parking Information

Recommendation: For all three days, it's recommended to leave your car at the designated parking area as the conference location at Marka Marulića 5, Pula only offers street parking with limited availability.

Due to the tourist season, the city is expected to be crowded, so it's advisable to park at the recommended parking area and walk to the conference venue.

Fratarski Island Information

Location: Google Maps - Fratarski Island

If you're planning to attend the activities on Fratarski Island on Day 3, please be sure to register for the boat transportation. Limited spots are available.

Those planning to stay overnight on the island should bring appropriate accommodations (tent, sleeping bag, etc.) and inform the organizers in advance.
